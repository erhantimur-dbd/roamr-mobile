FAQ

Common questions

How do I get started as an affiliate?

Fill out the application form below. We review applications within 24 hours. Once approved, you'll receive access to your dashboard and unique referral link.

When and how do I get paid?

Commissions are paid monthly, 30 days after the end of each month. We pay via bank transfer or PayPal. There's no minimum payout threshold.

Can I be both an affiliate and a business partner?

Yes, but we recommend choosing the programme that best fits your primary use case. Contact us if you need a custom arrangement.

What's the cookie window for referral tracking?

Our referral cookie lasts 30 days. If someone clicks your link and purchases within 30 days, you earn the commission.

Do I need a website to be an affiliate?

No. You can share your referral link on social media, YouTube, newsletters, or any other channel. A website helps but isn't required.

What are the requirements for business partners?

Business partners should be registered businesses with an existing customer base in the travel or hospitality industry. We'll discuss specific requirements during the application review.

Is there an API for integration?

Yes. Gold and Platinum business partners get full API access with documentation, sandbox environment, and technical support for integration.

Can I promote RoamR internationally?

Absolutely. Our eSIMs work for travellers visiting UK, Europe, and Turkiye from anywhere in the world. Your audience can be global.
