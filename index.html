FAQ

Common questions

What is an eSIM?

An eSIM (embedded SIM) is a digital SIM built into your phone. Instead of inserting a physical card, you scan a QR code and your phone connects to a mobile network. It's the same technology, just without the plastic.

How quickly will I receive my eSIM?

Instantly. As soon as your purchase is confirmed, we email you a QR code. Scan it, follow the on-screen instructions, and you're connected in under 3 minutes.

Can I keep my existing number?

Yes. Your eSIM works alongside your existing SIM. Keep your home number for calls and texts, and use the RoamR eSIM for data and local calls â€” your phone handles both.

Is there a fair usage policy?

All plans come with clearly stated allowances. There are no hidden throttling or fair usage surprises. What you see is what you get.
